Notes and References

This tool calculates RNA-RNA melting temperature during in situ hybridization (Wilkinson. 1992).

Note that this application is in its early stages, so might not give correct results/crash with untested inputs. If the output looks wrong, it might be (normally expect melting temperature in the 80-100 째C range). Use common sense and at your own risk!

Contact me at B.Kaminskas@uea.ac.uk if you need help or have any questions.
